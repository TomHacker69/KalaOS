# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors, maintainers, artists, and community members pledge to make participation in the KalaOS community a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

KalaOS exists to empower creativity, preserve artistic intent, recognize effort, and prioritize human dignity. We are committed to fostering an environment where creators and contributors can collaborate openly and respectfully.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

* Treating others with respect and professionalism.
* Providing constructive feedback.
* Respecting artistic expression and creative diversity.
* Giving proper credit for contributions and creative work.
* Supporting contributors and artists of all experience levels.
* Encouraging collaboration, learning, and innovation.
* Supporting the growth of the project and, if you find it valuable, considering giving the repository a ⭐ star.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.
* Personal attacks, intimidation, or trolling.
* Plagiarism or unauthorized use of creative work.
* Misattribution of contributions or artwork.
* Publishing private information without permission.
* Any conduct that creates a hostile or unwelcoming environment.

## Creative Integrity

Contributors are expected to:

* Respect creator ownership and attribution.
* Avoid submitting stolen, plagiarized, or unauthorized content.
* Promote transparency when using AI-assisted creative workflows.
* Preserve the values of artistic dignity, authenticity, and fair recognition.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing community standards and may take appropriate action when necessary.

Maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including repositories, discussions, issues, pull requests, documentation, community channels, and any public spaces where contributors represent KalaOS.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers through official project communication channels.

All reports will be reviewed and handled fairly and respectfully.
